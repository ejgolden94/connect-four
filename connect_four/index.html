How To Play Connect Four

Players take turns placing disks in lowest open slot in any column. The first player to get four disks in a row in any direction (horizontally, vertically or diagonally) wins the game! If all slots are filled and no one has gotten four disks in a row, the players tie!

To start a new game, hit Start New Game.
To play Connect Five or Connect Six hit Increase Board Size.
To return to a smaller board size, hit Decrease Board Size.
